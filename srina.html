Srina

Redefining tea-drinking & healthy living

Overview

Srina develops and distributes organically-grown tea from Sri Lanka. Through the sale of tea, Srina empowers tea farmers and cultivators by providing them with sustainable opportunities through a socially-conscious business model, and cultivates a community of tea drinkers passionate about health and wellness.

I worked with a team of designers to redefine the brand identity of Srina. I created digital assets, designed and developed its e-commerce website, and crafted targeted brand content.

Period & Timeframe

June 2016 — August 2016
7 weeks

Skills

Branding & Identity, Visual Design

Tools

Pen & Paper, Photoshop, Illustrator

Branding & Identity

Preliminary brand identity concepts

I was very excited to be given the opportunity to work with a social enterprise and bring its brand to life. Throughout the course of the project, we worked directly with the CEO. I pitched a range of ideas for the renewed brand identity as part of a parallel prototyping process, before coming together with my team to evaluate and combine our ideas.

Our refreshed brand identity conveys the simplicity and beauty of brewing and drinking tea, using earthy and neutral tones to create a natural and organic look & feel. Also, we created a series of thematic and expressive photography to tell the story of Srina tea and its business model. Overall, we wanted to define Srina as more than just tea, but a brand that creates human and environmental value, and invites consumers to participate in a culture of living healthily and sustainably together.
